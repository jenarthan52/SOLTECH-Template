# SOLTECH Template Documentation

## 1. Project Title

**SOLTECH – Modern Business Landing Page Template**

---

## 2. Introduction

SOLTECH is a modern, responsive, and professional one-page business website template designed for startups, SaaS products, digital agencies, and technology-based service companies.

This template helps businesses create a strong online presence by presenting their services, brand identity, customer trust, and business solutions in a clean and attractive layout.

The website is designed with a user-friendly structure and includes multiple important business sections such as a hero banner, services, integrations, testimonials, FAQ, contact form, and footer.

---

## 3. Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to design and develop a professional business landing page that can be used by startups, agencies, and service companies to promote their services online.

### Objectives:
- To create a modern and responsive business website
- To showcase digital services in a professional way
- To improve customer engagement using clear UI sections
- To build trust through testimonials and brand visibility
- To generate leads using a contact / get started form

---
